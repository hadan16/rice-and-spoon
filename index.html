ABOUT

Rice and Spoon is a family owned restaurant curated by Mama Tran and her son. The restaurant was developed to share the family's favorite home cooking recipes and spoon licking dishes made by Mama Tran. Rice and Spoon believes that great food brings indviduals together no matter how far you are or how busy you are in life. Life is too short to miss on special moments with your family, so our motto has always been “live to eat and not eat to live”. Our restaurant presents a chic yet modern-rustic feel, and the ambience is designed to reflect our current home living— relaxing, comforting and inviting with tasty food. We want bring the yummiest and freshest ingredients into our menu, yet stay authentic to Vietnamese food to the community. Thank you for visiting our page and we hope to have a visit from you soon!
