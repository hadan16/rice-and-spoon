About

Rice and Spoon is a family owned restaurant by mama Tran and her middle son with the help of her two daughters. Our restaurant was developed to share our favorite home cooking and spoon licking dishes made by our mama. We believe that great food really does bring people together no matter how far you are or how busy you are in life. Life is too short to miss on special moments with your family, so our motto has always been “live to eat and not eat to live”. Our restaurant presents a chic yet modern-rustic feel, and the ambience is designed to reflect our current home living— relaxing, comforting and inviting with tasty food. We want bring the yummiest and freshest ingredients into our menu yet stay authentic to Vietnamese food to the community.
